2 nabbed for kidnap attempt on 6 kids in Pasig City

Authorities rescued six minors from an attempted kidnapping by two suspects along the West Bank Road in Pasig City, Vonne Aquino reported in "24 Oras Weekend" on Sunday. advertisement Authorities said one victim, a 14-year-old, was lured by a suspect by an offer of milk tea. The teenager had nine and 11-year-old siblings, said Barangay Rosario Violence Against Women and Children-Barangay Council on the Protection for Children Desk Officer Janet Galvez. Galvez added that the suspects also kidnapped five and two-year-old children who were under the care of the teenager and another 14-year-old teenager who was the victim's friend. According to the National Capital Region Police Office, the police on Friday night stopped a taxi at a checkpoint when they saw one of the victims.

PHL athletes arrive in France for training camp ahead of Paris Olympics

Some of the Filipino athletes who have qualified to the Paris Olympics arrived in France on Sunday (Philippine time) for their one-month training camp. advertisement The Philippine Olympic Committee shared the development on its social media page. Athletes who flew to Metz were flag bearers Nesthy Petecio and Carlo Paalam, fellow boxers Hergie Bacyadan and Aira Villegas, weightlifters Elreen Ando, John Ceniza, and Vanessa Sarno, and rower Joanie Delgaco.
